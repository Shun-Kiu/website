tips to help!

1.know how to switch hands.

2.always plan your next move.

3.Always try to hit the shuttle away from your opponent and make your opponent move around the court.

4.A player should not reach over the net to hit the shuttlecock as that would be not allowed.

5.dont wait until the opponent hits the shuttlecock back then you move,

that way you could be delayed and potentially lose a point.
