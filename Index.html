badminton facts and tips

badminton is a sport which requires a racket and a shuttlecock.

it was invented in mid 1800s and developed in british india.

its a competitive game played in singles and doubles, in order to win you need to win points by hitting a shuttlecock across the

net and into your opponent's court forcing your opponent to make an error and be unable to return the shuttlecock back.
